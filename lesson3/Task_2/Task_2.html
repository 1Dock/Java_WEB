Khabib to Conor: "It is the same thing"

It's the same thing as the previous fight. If it's a first round finish, I'd back Conor. From the second and further, then Dustin wins.

The Russian - who retired in March with a record of 29-0 with eight knockout - has submission victories over both McGregor and Poirier, so he knows a thing two about their game plans and skillets.

He memorably scaled the Octagon at UFC 229 after defeating McGregor to attack his cornerman Dillon Danis,who had reportedly shouted insults at Khabib following the fight. The Russian has turned to promoting following his retirement and is mentoring fellow lightweight Islam Makhachev - currently #9 in the UFC rankings.
